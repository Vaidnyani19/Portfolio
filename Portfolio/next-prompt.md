A sleek testimonials page for the portfolio showcasing client and colleague endorsements.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Theme: Dark, futuristic, cyberpunk-inspired, high-tech portfolio
- Background: Deep void black (#0a0a0a) with charcoal surfaces (#1a1a2e)
- Primary Accent: Vivid neon cyan (#00f6ff) for CTAs, links, highlights
- Secondary Accent: Electric blue (#0088ff) for gradients and hover transitions
- Text Primary: Pure white (#ffffff) for headlines
- Text Secondary: Soft silver (#b0b0b0) for body copy
- Font: Space Grotesk, geometric sans-serif, modern and techy
- Buttons: 8px rounded corners, cyan background, glow on hover
- Cards: 8px rounded corners, charcoal background, subtle cyan border glow on hover
- Layout: Centered content, max-width container, generous whitespace, grid-based
- No harsh shadows — use subtle cyan-tinted glows for depth and elevation

**Page Structure:**
1. **Header:** Navigation with logo, Home, About, Experience, Skills, Projects, Blog, Contact
2. **Hero Section:** Title "What People Say" with a brief intro
3. **Testimonials Grid:** Cards with avatar, name, role, company, and quote text
4. **Call to Action:** "Want to work together?" with contact button
5. **Footer:** GitHub and LinkedIn links, copyright
