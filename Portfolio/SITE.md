## 2. Visual Language
*Strictly adhere to these descriptive rules when prompting Stitch.*

* **The "Vibe" (Adjectives):**

    * *Secondary:* **Clean** (Spacious layouts, clear hierarchy, breathing room).
    * *Tertiary:* **Bold** (Strong headlines, vivid accents, commanding presence).

* **Color Philosophy (Semantic):**
    * **Backgrounds:** Deep void black (#0a0a0a) with charcoal surfaces (#1a1a2e).
    * **Accents:** Vivid neon cyan (#00f6ff) for interactive elements and highlights.
    * **Text:** Pure white (#ffffff) for headlines, soft silver (#b0b0b0) for body.
