# Design System: Portfolio
**Project ID:** 845243756804848155

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
A sleek, futuristic, and immersive dark-mode portfolio. The atmosphere is **bold**, **high-tech**, and **cyberpunk-inspired** — featuring deep dark backgrounds punctuated by vivid neon cyan accents. The overall feeling is of a polished developer showcase with a commanding, modern aesthetic. Layouts are clean and spacious with a gallery-like presentation.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **Void Black** (#0a0a0a) – Primary background, creating depth and contrast
- **Deep Charcoal** (#1a1a2e) – Secondary background, card surfaces, elevated containers
- **Neon Cyan** (#00f6ff) – Primary accent for CTAs, highlights, links, and interactive elements
- **Electric Blue** (#0088ff) – Secondary accent for gradient transitions and hover states
- **Pure White** (#ffffff) – Primary text color for headlines and important content
- **Soft Silver** (#b0b0b0) – Secondary text for body copy and descriptions
- **Muted Gray** (#6b7280) – Tertiary text for metadata, timestamps, and subtle labels

## 3. Typography Rules
- **Font Family:** Space Grotesk — a geometric sans-serif with a techy, modern character
- **Headline Weight:** Bold (700) with tight letter-spacing for impact
- **Body Weight:** Regular (400) with comfortable line-height for readability
- **Accent Text:** Medium (500) for navigation, labels, and buttons
- **Size Scale:** Large headlines (48-72px), medium subheads (24-32px), comfortable body (16-18px)

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** Rounded corners (8px), neon cyan background with dark text, subtle glow effect on hover. Ghost/outline variants with cyan border for secondary actions.
* **Cards/Containers:** Rounded corners (8px), deep charcoal background (#1a1a2e), subtle border with low-opacity cyan. Whisper-soft shadow with cyan tint on hover for depth.
* **Inputs/Forms:** Dark background with subtle border, rounded corners (8px), cyan accent on focus state. Clean, minimal styling.
* **Navigation:** Transparent header with blur backdrop, clean horizontal nav with active state indicated by cyan underline or glow.
* **Tags/Badges:** Pill-shaped (full rounded), small text, semi-transparent cyan background.

## 5. Layout Principles
- **Centered content** with max-width container (1200-1400px)
- **Generous whitespace** between sections for breathing room
- **Full-width hero sections** with background effects
- **Grid-based layouts** for cards and project showcases
- **Responsive design** prioritizing desktop-first
